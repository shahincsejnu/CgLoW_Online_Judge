Ashik got admitted into Department of CSEJnU. His goal is to find his true love. So he is trying to offer his love all 
his batchmate. He takes all of his friend merit list and chose a merit value randomly. He comes to an interesting
decision that,is the merit value in even position or not? if it is even he will propose that girl otherwise he will not.


input
In first line there will be two integer n and x. n denotes the number of girls in ashiks class. 
x denotes the merit that he choose. The next line will be n merit position of his batchmate

output
print "yes" if he make a propose else print "no"

Input : 
3 2
2 3 1

Output :
no

Input :
5 2
5 4 1 2 3
 
Output :
yes
